# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

NutriBalance ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the NutriBalance mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you use NutriBalance, you may provide us with:

- **Personal Information**: Name, email address (optional)
- **Health Data**: Weight, height, age, activity level, dietary goals
- **Nutrition Data**: Food entries, drink entries, meal logs, nutritional information
- **Progress Data**: Weight history, achievements, streaks

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, frequency of use (only if analytics are enabled)
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information about app crashes (only if enabled)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Track your nutrition and health progress
- Generate personalized insights and recommendations
- Create PDF reports for healthcare providers
- Improve the App's features and user experience
- Send notifications (only if you enable them)

## 3. Data Storage

### 3.1 Local Storage

**All your personal data is stored locally on your device.** NutriBalance uses Core Data for persistent storage, which means:

- Your data remains on your iPhone
- We do not have access to your data
- Your data is not uploaded to any servers

### 3.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

- Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
- Syncing uses your Apple ID and iCloud storage
- Apple's privacy policies apply to iCloud-stored data
- You can disable sync at any time in Settings

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Third-Party Services

NutriBalance may integrate with:

- **Apple HealthKit** (optional): If you grant permission, the App can read/write weight and nutrition data to Apple Health. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
- **PDF Export**: When you export reports, the data leaves the App in the format you choose (PDF, CSV). You control who receives these exports.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

- Data is stored in the iOS secure container
- Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection
- No transmission of personal data over networks (unless iCloud sync is enabled)
- Encrypted iCloud storage (if enabled)

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

### 6.1 Access Your Data
View all your data within the App at any time.

### 6.2 Export Your Data
Export your complete data in PDF or CSV format from Settings > Export Data.

### 6.3 Delete Your Data
Delete all your data by:
- Deleting individual entries within the App
- Using "Reset All Data" in Settings
- Deleting the App from your device

### 6.4 Modify Your Data
Edit any entries or personal information at any time within the App.

## 7. Children's Privacy

NutriBalance is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice within the App
- Providing a new version through the App Store

## 9. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

- You delete specific entries
- You reset all data in Settings
- You delete the App

If iCloud sync is enabled, data is retained according to your iCloud settings.

## 10. International Users

NutriBalance is developed in the Netherlands and complies with EU data protection regulations (GDPR). If you are located outside the EU:

- Your data remains on your local device
- No cross-border data transfer occurs (unless iCloud sync is enabled)
- iCloud transfers are governed by Apple's data handling practices

## 12. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have additional rights:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, use the App's built-in features or contact us directly.

## 13. App Store Requirements

This App complies with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines regarding privacy:

- We do not collect data for tracking across apps
- We do not share data with data brokers
- We provide clear disclosure of data practices
- We offer data deletion capabilities
